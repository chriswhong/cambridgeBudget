About this Visualization

This Visualization shows the City of Philadelphia's General Fund Budget for Fiscal Year 2012. The bubbles represent the approved budget amount for each subcategory. Hovering over a subcategory name will highlight its corresponding bubble on the chart. Likewise, hovering over a bubble will highlight it's corresponding subcategory name.
